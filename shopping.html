Shop Local

Kinsale has a great variety of shops, from great local boutiques, markets and clothing stores for all your clothing needs to patisseries, bakeries and butchers for all your food needs. Kinsale also has many supermarkets for all general goods needs.

Groceries

Kinsale has a great selection of markets, shops and supermarkets to shopping for groceries, listed below are a selection.

  • Smith's Supervalu Kinsale

    Smith’s SuperValu Kinsale is an award winning supermarket located in The Glen, Kinsale. With a full product offering this store specialises in fresh foods with an instore bakery, fresh meat, fresh fish, delicatessen and produce depts. Follow us on Facebook for regular updates and details of our great offers and promotions. Super Valu Kinsale also offers a home delivery service and you can shop on-line by visiting www.supervalu.ie

  • Spar Kinsale

    Spar Kinsale is centrally located in kinsale town centre on Pearse street. Spar offers a wide variety of foods, drinks and and other offerings, with a Subway™ located in store with a seating area located at the front of the store.
