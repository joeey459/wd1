Need somewhere to rest your head? Well, Kinsale has a great selection of hotels and B&Bs to stay in. For a coprehensive list of accommodation see below.

Acton's Hotel Kinsale

Pier Road, Kinsale, Co. Cork, P17 PX80, Ireland
Tel: 021 477 9900
www.actonshotelkinsale.com

This 4 star hotel overlooks Kinsale Harbour & yacht club allowing you to wake up to the twinkling sound of yacht masts. After a substantial renovation in 2012 the hotel re opened in March 2013.

From the incredible location right on the water front set in generous lawns to the beautifully refurbished ballroom, Actons Hotel is an ideal venue for a wedding that family and friends will never forget.

Over the years Actons of Kinsale has established a reputation for its exceptional location, highly personal and genuine guest care, and renowned cuisine. Actons has become a ‘home away from home’ for both the people of Kinsale and the business and holiday travellers who know, feel and appreciate, the Actons of Kinsale difference.

It is proud to be a founding member of the Kinsale Good Food Circle and a member of the Kinsale Chamber of Tourism.

This contemporary boutique style hotel comprises 74 en suite bedrooms including a bridal and family suites, Sidney’s Bar & Brassiere offer all day dining, Health & Leisure Club with fully equipped gym, 15 meter swimming pool, sauna & steam room and 3 treatment rooms.

Ballinacurra House Kinsale

Tel: 021 477 9040
info@ballinacurra.com | www.ballinacurra.com
Ballinacurra, Kinsale, Co. Cork, P17 YF76

Ballinacurra House, Kinsale was voted Ireland’s Premier Exclusive use Estate and a venue that is offering a totally different private house party experience for prospective couples, looking for something other than a traditional packaged ”factory” wedding.

The award winning 40-acre Georgian property is only estate 2 minutes from Kinsale – Ireland’s Ultimate Wedding Destination — with its famed harbour, forts and culinary reputation and offers bespoke private weddings with a difference.

It also attracts a stellar list of international clients and over the past year has hosted the Heads of State, CEOs of World Aerospace and the top executives from Fashion Avenue New York, King of Pop Michael Jackson. Early 2014 Kim Kardashian and Kanye West tried to book their honeymoon at the estate but were rebuffed as the venue was already booked !

At Ballinacurra House customers can enjoy the experience of a 40 acre luxury, private country residence, complete with Award Winning personal chef David Rice, – a bespoke wedding of your choice, in the inspirational setting of renowned medieval tourist town — Kinsale, -which has a “destination wedding” feel and also the culinary capital with world class activities and over 60 bars and restaurants .
